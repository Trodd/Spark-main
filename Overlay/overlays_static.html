Spark Overlays

The overlays provided here are free for non-commercial use with attribution. If you wish to have a customized set of overlays for your league, contact NtsFranz for more information.

  • Home
  • Overlays

A scene collection with these overlays has already been added to OBS. You can also add these URLs as browser sources to your streaming software yourself.

  • /branding

     Several examples of acceptable attribution. You can create your own, or crop one of the examples on this page.

  • /overlays/match_setup

     Configure team names and logos manually.

  • /overlays/caster_setup

     Configure caster names and logos for casting overlays.

  • /midmatch_overlay

     An overlay that covers the default ORANGE TEAM and BLUE TEAM banners with automatically generated team names from VRML team lists. Also shows neutral and defensive joust times. Customize the team names and logos in Spark's Settings->Overlay window.

  • /scoreboard

     A recreation of the default scoreboard shown at the end of matches. This includes data for players that have dropped during the course of the match.

  • /configurable_overlay

     A full overlay replacement. Hide the default overlay when using this. Customize the team names and logos in Spark's Settings->Overlay window.

  • /combat_overlay

     A simple combat overlay showing player name plates for both teams. Displays player names, numbers, possession status (glowing indicator), and stunned states. Perfect for OBS streaming.

  • Combat Overlays

    Overlays specifically designed for Combat mode.

    • /combat_payload.html

       Team headers for Payload mode (no player nameplates).

    • /combat_capturepoint.html

       Team headers for Capture Point mode (no player nameplates).

    • /combat_full_overlay.html

       Full combat overlay with additional features.

    • /combat_scoreboard.html

       Combat scoreboard display.

  • Casting Overlays

    Overlays for casting and streaming.

    • /countdown_timer.html

       Countdown timer for stream breaks.

    • /team_roster.html

       Team roster display.

    • /team_vs_team.html

       Team vs team display.

    • /stream_start_screen.html

       Stream starting soon screen.

  • /speedometer/player

     A simple speed reading overlay for the client player speed. The text is in white, so it won't immediately be visible in your browser.

  • /speedometer/disc

     A simple speed reading overlay for the disc speed. The text is in white, so it won't immediately be visible in your browser.

  • /speedometer/lone_echo_1

     A simple speed reading overlay for the player speed in Lone Echo. The text is in white, so it won't immediately be visible in your browser.

  • /speedometer/lone_echo_2

     A simple speed reading overlay for the player speed in Lone Echo II. The text is in white, so it won't immediately be visible in your browser.
